Target Audience:

Geolocation - Where do they live?

Since our location is close to the campus at Utah Tech, most people will probably be from around Utah area and the audience will likely live in urban or suburban areas, close to universities, colleges, or large business districts.

Age - What is the age range?

Our target audience will be primarily students between the ages of 18-30 and young couples aged 22-35.

Gender - What is the gender of the majority of users?

We want both male and females.

Other Demographics

We probably would want anyone seeking to find a place to stay within Saint George regardless of their geographical location or ethnic background. We are running a business not a political campaign.
