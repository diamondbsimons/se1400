Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

The website will contain details about the my airbnb business and properties, including photos of the rooms and common areas, pricing, availability, guest reviews, and nearby attractions. It will also feature a booking system for guests.

Where will you get all of the content for your site (i.e., images & text)?

The content will be sourced from our Airbnb property, including professional photos of the property, text about amenities and nearby attractions, as well as guest testimonials and feedback from past stays.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

Students and couples looking for affordable, comfortable, and well-located housing will benefit from this site. It provides an affordable way to find, explore, and book a stay at a well-maintained property that meets their needs.

Why should a site be built for this?

A website allows for better control over marketing and communication with guests. It also provides an easier way to directly manage data and the property, and makes everything look more professional than us as the business solely relying on third-party platforms like Airbnb.

What would you like users to do at your site?

We want users to explore the website and have it be easy to find the property, check availability, view pricing, read guest reviews, and find a place or room to stay directly through the website.

What is the goal of the site?

The goal of the site is to grow our brand and help bring capital to our business through networking online.
